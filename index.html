Welcome to the Karate Club

Proudly serving the Comox military community since 1997, we are dedicated to helping you forge unbreakable confidence, physical strength, and real-world self-defense skills.

The Art of Okinawa Goju Ryu

Unlike modern sport-focused martial arts, the Comox Traditional Karate Club teaches classical Okinawa Goju Ryu Karate-Do. Known as the "hard and soft" style, our training prioritizes practical self-defense, situational awareness, and deep personal development.

Beyond the physical workout, traditional karate is a powerful tool for sharpening the mind. Our dojo focuses on building resilience, deep concentration, and a quiet, grounded self-confidence that carries over into everyday life.

Master Traditional Weapons

Once students have built a strong foundation and successfully passed their first grading, training expands to include traditional Okinawan weapon forms, including the Bo (staff), Sai, and Tonfa.

A Safe, Professional Environment

Your safety and privacy are our top priorities. To ensure the highest standard of instruction:

  • All instructors have passed comprehensive criminal background checks.
  • Our teaching team is proudly High Five certified.

Note: As a physical activity, all members are required to sign a standard liability waiver and provide confidential health/contact information upon joining.

Try It For Free

Martial arts is a personal journey, and we want to make sure our dojo is the perfect fit for you. Prospective students are invited to join us for two free classes (one full week of training) absolutely risk-free.
