Classes

Our Class Schedule

We offer a structured class program focusing on traditional technique, sparring, and practical self-defense for both adults and juniors. Prospective students are welcome to attend two free classes (one week) to see if our style is the right fit. Please wear loose clothing or a Gi if you have one!

Adults

  • Tuesdays & Thursdays: 1800 - 2000 hrs
  • Saturdays: 1030 - 1200 hrs (Weapons Training: 1200 - 1300 hrs)

Juniors (Ages 8 - 11)

  • Thursdays: 1800 - 1900 hrs
  • Saturdays: 1030 - 1130 hrs

Winter Beginners Session

Ready to start your journey? Join our specialized Winter Beginners Session, instructed by our 6th, 4th, and 2nd Degree Black Belts!

  • Session Dates: January 7th to March 27th (First class starts Jan 9th)
  • Location: 19 Wing Fitness & Community Centre (Multipurpose Rooms B & C)
  • Eligibility: Open to all Military personnel, dependents, DND employees, and their families (Ages 7 to Adult).
