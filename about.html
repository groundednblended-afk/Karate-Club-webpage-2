About the Club

Why Join the 19 Wing Karate Club?

Our dojo combines deep martial arts discipline with a welcoming, fitness-focused community. Whether you are stepping onto the mats for the very first time or looking to sharpen advanced skills, our dedicated coaches support every level with clear goals and positive training.

What We Offer

  • Structured Progression: Clear, goal-oriented belt advancements under the traditional Goju Ryu system.
  • Community Training: A friendly, supportive group environment for all ages.
  • Events & Seminars: Regular tournaments, demonstrations, and special events across Vancouver Island.
  • Professional Instruction: High Five certified instructors holding comprehensive background checks.
